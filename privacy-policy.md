# Privacy Policy for Snake Escape

**Last Updated: January 14, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Snake Escape**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game on web platforms such as CrazyGames and Poki.

## 2. Information We Collect
Snake Escape does **not** directly collect, store, or share any personal data (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

However, the game utilizes third-party SDKs and advertising partners that may automatically collect certain non-personal information, including:
*   **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and browser version.
*   **Log Data**: IP address, access times, and gameplay statistics.
*   **Identifiers**: Unique advertising identifiers provided by your device/browser.

## 3. How Information is Used
The information collected by third-party services is used to:
*   Deliver and optimize in-game advertisements (Rewarded and Interstitial).
*   Provide analytics to help us improve gameplay and performance.
*   Ensure the security and technical integrity of the game.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Snake Escape is distributed through gaming platforms that use their own SDKs for ads and analytics. These include:
*   **CrazyGames**: [CrazyGames Privacy Policy](https://www.crazygames.com/privacy)
*   **Poki**: [Poki Privacy Policy](https://poki.com/en/privacy)

These third parties may use cookies and other tracking technologies to provide tailored advertising and analyze usage.

## 5. Cookies and Local Storage
We use **Local Storage** within your browser to save your game progress (levels completed, XP, and rank). This data remains on your local device and is not transmitted to our servers. Third-party platforms may use cookies to manage ad frequency and session data.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Snake Escape is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe such data has been collected by a third-party partner, please contact us.

## 7. Data Security
We and our partners implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 8. Your Rights
Depending on your location (such as the USA or India), you may have rights regarding your data, including the right to:
*   Request information about the data collected.
*   Request deletion of certain data held by third-party partners.
*   Opt-out of personalized advertising through your browser or device settings.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
